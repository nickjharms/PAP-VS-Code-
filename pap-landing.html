About the Program

What Is the Patient Adherence Program?

PAP is a structured, evidence-based program designed to help patients follow through on their prescribed treatment plans. Non-adherence is one of the leading causes of preventable illness and hospitalizations — we're here to change that.

👤
Personalized Care Plans
Each patient receives a customized plan tailored to their condition, lifestyle, and goals.
📱
Digital Check-Ins & Reminders
Automated medication reminders, appointment alerts, and symptom tracking via app or SMS.
🎓
Patient Education
Condition-specific educational resources, videos, and one-on-one coaching sessions.
📊
Progress Monitoring
Regular health assessments and real-time reporting shared with your care team.
Who We Serve

Patient Categories

Our program supports patients across six major care areas, each with specialized protocols and dedicated coordinators.

💓
Chronic Disease Management
Patients managing long-term conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and COPD. Our program provides continuous monitoring, medication management, and lifestyle coaching to prevent complications.
Diabetes Hypertension Heart Disease COPD
🧠
Mental Health & Behavioral
Support for patients with depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and substance use disorders. We combine medication adherence with behavioral coaching and mental wellness resources.
Depression Anxiety Bipolar Substance Use
🎗️
Oncology & Cancer Care
Patients undergoing cancer treatment, including chemotherapy, immunotherapy, and radiation. We support adherence to complex regimens and help manage side effects and emotional wellbeing.
Chemotherapy Immunotherapy Radiation Survivorship
🦴
Post-Surgical Recovery
Patients recovering from major surgeries who require adherence to rehabilitation protocols, wound care, medication schedules, and follow-up appointments to prevent complications.
Orthopedic Cardiac Bariatric Rehab

Senior & Geriatric Care
Older adults managing multiple medications and comorbidities. We provide simplified care plans, caregiver coordination, fall-risk assessments, and cognitive support to ensure safety and adherence.
Polypharmacy Dementia Fall Prevention Caregiver Support
🫁
Respiratory & Pulmonary
Patients with asthma, COPD, sleep apnea, and other respiratory conditions. Inhaler technique training, environmental trigger management, and adherence to pulmonary rehab programs.
Asthma Sleep Apnea Pulmonary Rehab Inhaler Training
The Process

How the Program Works

From enrollment to ongoing support — here's your journey with PAP.

1
Enroll & Assess
Complete a brief intake form and health assessment. Our care team reviews your conditions, medications, and goals within 24 hours.
2
Build Your Plan
A dedicated care coordinator creates your personalized adherence plan, including medication schedules, educational resources, and check-in cadence.
3

Receive daily reminders, track your symptoms, and connect with your care team via our app, phone, or telehealth video visits.

Track & Improve
Monthly progress reviews with your care coordinator. Your plan evolves as your health improves, keeping you on the path to better outcomes.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the Patient Adherence Program.

What is patient adherence and why does it matter?
▾
Patient adherence refers to how consistently a patient follows their prescribed treatment plan — including taking medications on time, attending appointments, and making lifestyle changes. Research shows that non-adherence leads to 125,000 preventable deaths and $300 billion in healthcare costs annually in the U.S. alone. Our program exists to close that gap.
How do I enroll in the program?
▾
Enrollment is simple — fill out our online form below or call our care team. You'll complete a short health assessment, and within 24 hours a care coordinator will contact you to confirm eligibility and build your personalized plan. Your doctor's referral is welcome but not required to start.
Is the program covered by my insurance?
▾
Yes — PAP is covered by most major commercial insurance plans, Medicare Part B, and Medicaid in participating states. For uninsured or underinsured patients, we offer a sliding-scale financial assistance program. Contact our billing team for a no-cost benefits check before enrolling.
Will PAP replace my current doctor or care team?
▾
No. PAP is designed to complement — not replace — your existing care relationships. Our coordinators work alongside your physicians, specialists, and pharmacists to ensure everyone is aligned. We share progress reports with your care team only with your consent.
How much time does the program require from me?
▾
Most patients spend 5–15 minutes per week on program activities — logging medications, reviewing reminders, and completing brief check-ins. Monthly 30-minute coordinator calls are included. Everything is designed to fit into your existing routine with minimal disruption.
What happens if I miss a dose or skip a check-in?
▾
No judgement — life happens. Our system will send a follow-up reminder and your care coordinator will reach out if they notice a pattern. The goal is support, not surveillance. We work with you to understand barriers and find practical solutions, not penalize you for missing steps.
Can family members or caregivers be involved?
▾
Absolutely. With your permission, we can include a designated caregiver or family member in your care plan, give them access to reminders, and involve them in coordinator calls. A strong support network is one of the biggest predictors of long-term adherence success.
