About the Program

What Is the Patient Adherence Program?

PAP is a structured, evidence-based program designed to help patients consistently follow their prescribed treatment plans. Non-adherence causes 125,000 preventable deaths and $300 billion in excess costs annually — we're here to change that.

👤
Personalized Care Plans
Each patient receives a customized plan built around their condition, lifestyle, and goals.
📱
Smart Reminders & Digital Check-Ins
Automated medication alerts, appointment reminders, and symptom tracking via app or SMS.
🎓
Patient Education
Condition-specific resources, videos, and one-on-one coaching to build health literacy.
📊
Progress Monitoring
Regular health assessments and real-time reporting shared with your entire care team.
Who We Serve

Patient Categories

Six specialized care areas, each with dedicated coordinators and condition-specific protocols.

💓

Chronic Disease Management

Long-term conditions including diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and COPD. Continuous monitoring, medication management, and lifestyle coaching to prevent complications.

Diabetes Hypertension Heart Disease COPD
🧠

Mental Health & Behavioral

Depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and substance use. Combines medication adherence with behavioral coaching and mental wellness resources for whole-person care.

Depression Anxiety Bipolar Substance Use
🎗️

Oncology & Cancer Care

Patients undergoing chemotherapy, immunotherapy, or radiation. Complex regimen support, side-effect management, and emotional wellbeing throughout the treatment journey.

Chemotherapy Immunotherapy Radiation Survivorship
🦴

Post-Surgical Recovery

Adherence to rehab protocols, wound care, medication schedules, and follow-up appointments after major surgery to prevent complications and accelerate healing.

Orthopedic Cardiac Bariatric Rehab
👴

Senior & Geriatric Care

Older adults managing multiple medications and comorbidities. Simplified care plans, caregiver coordination, fall-risk assessments, and cognitive support tools.

Polypharmacy Dementia Fall Prevention Caregiver Support
🫁

Respiratory & Pulmonary

Asthma, COPD, sleep apnea, and other respiratory conditions. Inhaler technique training, environmental trigger management, and pulmonary rehab adherence support.

Asthma Sleep Apnea Pulmonary Rehab Inhaler Training
The Process

How the Program Works

From enrollment to ongoing support — your complete journey with PAP.

1

Enroll & Assess

Complete a brief intake and health assessment. Our care team reviews your case within 24 hours.

2

Build Your Plan

A dedicated coordinator creates your personalized adherence plan, medication schedule, and check-in cadence.

3

Stay Connected

Daily reminders, symptom tracking, and care team access via app, phone, or telehealth visits.

4

Track & Improve

Monthly progress reviews. Your plan evolves as your health improves, keeping you on the right path.

Important Patient Information

What to know before and during enrollment.

Open to patients 18+ with a confirmed diagnosis in one of our six care categories and an active relationship with a participating provider.

Privacy & HIPAA

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the Patient Adherence Program.

Patient adherence means consistently following your prescribed treatment — taking medications on time, attending appointments, and making lifestyle changes.
